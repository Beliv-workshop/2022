Call for Papers (preliminary)

BELIV 2018 is a forum to discuss research methods in visualization, spanning novel and not-yet fully established evaluation methods for visualization, to methods for more broadly establishing the validity and scope of acquired visualization knowledge. The 2018 call for papers will include an expanded scope from years past to reflect this newly broadened purview. Complementary to the broader scope, 2018 will also include a focus topic — the replication crisis and its effect on VIS — which will shape half of the workshop. The call for papers will thus also include specifics around this topic, including issues related to both quantitative and qualitative work, as well as opportunities for reflective assessment of unsuccessful replication efforts.
